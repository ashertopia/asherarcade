Our Policies

Plain-English terms, refunds, and privacy for everything Asher Arcade makes.

Who we are

Asher Arcade (asherarcade.com) is operated by Scott Asher LLC. All orders, payments, and services on this site — and Asher Arcade listings on marketplaces like Etsy — are provided by Scott Asher LLC. Questions? Use the contact form and we'll get back to you within 24 hours.

Orders, deposits & payment

  • Custom games and event tools are quoted from our published pricing ($79–$299 tiers). Nothing is owed until you approve the quote.
  • An optional deposit locks in your build date and is applied in full to your total.
  • Deposits are fully refundable any time before we start building. Once building starts, the deposit covers work performed.

Refunds & our guarantee

  • If your product doesn't work at your event and we can't make it right, you get a full refund. That's the whole policy. Tell us within 7 days of your event.
  • Custom builds include a revision round before your event. We test every build on real phones before delivery.
  • Digital and personalized products (including Etsy orders) aren't returnable once delivered, but the guarantee above still applies — if it doesn't work, we fix it or refund it.
  • Etsy orders are additionally covered by Etsy's purchase protection.

What you get & what you can do with it

  • Your custom game or tool is yours to share and play at your event and beyond. Hosting on asherarcade.com is included for the period stated in your tier ($20/yr renewal after the first year; 60 days for the Occasion tier).
  • We keep the right to show anonymized versions of games in our portfolio unless you ask us not to.
  • Our free tools (Quiz Builder, game demos) are provided as-is for personal use.

Privacy

  • Photo Drop photos go straight to your own Google Drive. We never store, view, or keep copies of your guests' photos on our systems.
  • Quiz Builder quizzes live entirely in the share link. There is no account and no server database of your quiz content.
  • The contact form collects your name, email, and message only so we can reply. We never sell or share your information.
  • This site uses Google Analytics to count visits. Leaderboards store only the display name a player chooses to enter.
  • To have any information removed, use the contact form.

The boring-but-necessary part

Products are provided as-is to the fullest extent allowed by law; Scott Asher LLC's total liability for any order is limited to the amount you paid for it. These policies may be updated over time — the version on this page is the one in effect.
