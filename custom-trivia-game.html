FAQ
Common questions
How many questions can I include?

The standard game includes up to 30 questions. More is easy — we can extend the game into rounds, add tie-breaker questions, or build a multi-night tournament. Just mention it when you reach out.

Can you build trivia around photos?

Yes. Send us the photos and the questions ("who is this?", "match the year", "which groomsman said this?") and we'll wire them in. Works great for weddings and family reunions.

How does the leaderboard work?

Every guest enters a name and plays. The leaderboard updates in real time and is visible to everyone — on phones, on a projector, or on a dedicated screen. It keeps the room engaged long after the first round ends.

Can guests play on their phones?

Yes. The game is browser-based and works on iOS, Android, desktop, and older phones. No app to install — guests tap the link and they're in.

How long does it take to build?

Most games are ready within 7 days of receiving your questions. Rush orders (3-day turnaround) are available — just mention it when you reach out.

What happens after 1 year?

The game keeps working as long as we host it. The first year of hosting is included; after that it's $20/year if you want to keep it live. If you don't, we just quietly retire the link.

Do you offer discounts for churches or nonprofits?

Yes. 50% off the first game for Christian churches, ministries, homeschool families, active military, veterans, and first responders. Honor system, no paperwork — just mention it.

Ready to stump your guests?

Send us your topic, your questions, and who the game's for. We'll come back in 7 days with a link that makes your people compete.
