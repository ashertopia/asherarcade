FAQ
Questions about birthday games
Can the game character actually look like the birthday person?

Yes. We generate custom artwork based on photos you provide. The character is stylized and recognizable — think cartoon version, not photorealistic.

What milestone birthdays work best?

30s, 40s, 50s, and 60s are the most popular — people want something funny and personal for the big ones. But we've built games for kids' birthdays, sweet sixteens, and 80th birthday parties too.

Can I give it as a gift even if I'm not throwing the party?

Absolutely. Many orders are gifts — someone commissions the game, the birthday person gets the link, and they share it with friends. The game lives on asherarcade.com with its own URL.

How long does it take?

Most games are ready within 7 days. Rush orders (3 days) are available if the birthday is coming up fast.

How long is the game hosted?

The Memory tier ($199+) includes 1 year of hosting, then $20/yr to renew. PhotoDrop is available for 60 days — enough time for everyone to download their party photos.
