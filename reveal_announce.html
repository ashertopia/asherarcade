FAQ
Questions about reveal puzzles
Can I use this for a gender reveal party?

Yes — it's one of the most popular uses. The puzzle can reveal a pink or blue image, an ultrasound, or any photo you choose. Guests solve it on their phones at the party, or from wherever they are.

Can I use this for an engagement or proposal announcement?

Absolutely. Share the link after the proposal and let friends and family race to uncover the engagement photo. It's a much more memorable way to share the news than a group text.

What does the puzzle actually reveal at the end?

Whatever you want. A photo, a baby name in text, a video, a QR code, or a link to a website. You provide the reveal — we build the delivery.

Can guests play on their phones at the party?

Yes. It's a browser link — no app, no account, no download. Guests tap the link, play, and the reveal unlocks when they finish.

Can the puzzle reveal a YouTube video or link to a website?

Yes. When the puzzle is solved, it can open a QR code, auto-play a YouTube video, or redirect to any URL — a registry, an engagement site, a Google Drive folder, anything you want.

How long is the game available?

The Occasion tier ($79) is hosted for 60 days. The Memory and Experience tiers include 1 year of hosting, and you can renew for $20/year after that.
