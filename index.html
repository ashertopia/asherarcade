FAQ
Common questions
Do guests need to download an app to play?

No. Every game is browser-based and works on any phone, tablet, or computer. Guests just tap the link — nothing to install, no account needed.

How long does it take to build?

Most games are ready within 7 days. Rush orders (3-day turnaround) are available — just mention it when you reach out and we'll let you know if the timeline works.

Can I see it before I pay?

You can play any of our live games right now to get a feel for the quality. For your commissioned game, we share a preview before final delivery — you don't pay the balance until you're happy with it.

What occasions work best?

Weddings, baby showers, gender reveals, birthdays, graduations, anniversaries, church events, youth groups, and office parties. If there's a person or a moment worth celebrating, we can build something around it.

How does the leaderboard work?

Every custom game includes a live leaderboard. Guests enter their name when they play and compete for the top score. It updates in real time and works on every device — no setup needed on your end.

What is PhotoDrop?

PhotoDrop is a guest photo collection app. Guests scan a QR code at your event and upload photos straight from their phone — no app needed. Photos are stored securely for 60 days so everyone can download their favorites.

Do you offer discounts for churches or nonprofits?

Yes. We offer 50% off the first game for Christian churches, ministries, homeschool families, active military, veterans, and first responders. Honor system, no paperwork — just mention it when you reach out.
